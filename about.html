About Us


Grey Shuga began baking confectionery products in low volume bakery facilities. The original handmade pastries, cakes, and sandwiches have always used the finest quality and 100% natural healthy ingredients: Highest quality flour, Fresh whole grade A eggs, butter creams and dairy products, pure cane sugar, dark and white chocolates, fresh fruit purees and fillings, the world's finest vanilla and essences.



Why Choose Us


Home baked goods have a special allure about them that store-bought commercially made products simply don’t match up to. They are lovingly made with care and use the best ingredients and methods – the difference is obvious in the final result.

Our Vision


To be a leading cake artistry brand known for its continuous innovative expression of art and creativity in edible forms.

Our Mission


To create deliciously edible works of art that celebrate life’s moments, nurturing creativity while remaining committed to supporting emerging artists and fostering creativity in our community.
